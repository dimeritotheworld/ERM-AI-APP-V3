‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What happens if I run out of AI queries mid-month?
A: You can still use all other features! You just won't be able to use AI assistance until the next month or until you upgrade to Pro for unlimited queries.
Q: Can I save unused queries for next month?
A: No, queries don't roll over. You get a fresh 50 queries on the 1st of each month (Free plan).
Q: Does editing AI-generated content use another query?
A: No! Once AI generates content, you can edit, copy, or reuse it without using additional queries.
Q: If I upgrade to Pro mid-month, what happens to my usage?
A: Your queries become unlimited immediately! Your previous usage count no longer matters.
Q: Can team members share the 50 queries?
A: The 50 query limit applies to the entire workspace, not per user. All team members share the same pool of queries.
Q: How accurate is the AI?
A: Our AI is highly accurate for risk management contexts, but always review and validate AI suggestions. Use your expertise and judgment for final decisions.
