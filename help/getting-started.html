Welcome to Dimeri ERM! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to start managing your organization's risks effectively. By the end of this guide, you'll have created your first risk, added controls, and generated a professional report.

Your First Login

When you first access Dimeri ERM, you'll be guided through a quick setup process:

1

Create Your Account

Enter your name, email, and create a secure password. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

2

Set Up Your Workspace

Name your organization and select your industry. This helps the AI provide relevant risk suggestions and templates.

3

Choose Your Role

Select your role (Risk Manager, Analyst, Executive, etc.) to customize your dashboard experience.

4

Start Managing Risks

You're ready! The dashboard will show you quick actions to create your first risk register.

Dashboard Tour

The dashboard is your command center for risk management. Here's what you'll find:

A

Sidebar Navigation

Quick access to Dashboard, Risk Register, Controls, Reports, Activity Log, Settings, and Help.

B

Risk Summary Cards

At-a-glance view of total risks, high-priority items, controls implemented, and overall risk reduction percentage.

C

Risk Heatmap

Visual representation of your risks plotted by likelihood and impact. Red zones indicate critical risks needing immediate attention.

D

Risk Comparison Chart

Compare inherent risk (before controls) vs. residual risk (after controls) to see the effectiveness of your risk management efforts.

E

Activity Feed

Recent actions taken in the system, including risk updates, control changes, and report generation.

Create Your First Risk

There are two ways to create a risk in Dimeri ERM:

‚úèÔ∏è

Manual Entry

Fill in each field yourself. Best when you have detailed information about the risk.

  1. Click Risk Register in sidebar
  2. Click Add New Risk
  3. Select Manual Entry
  4. Enter title, category, likelihood (1-5), and impact (1-5)
  5. Add description, consequences, and root causes
  6. Click Save Risk
‚ú®

Describe with AI

Recommended

Simply describe your concern in plain language. AI fills in all the details.

  1. Click Risk Register in sidebar
  2. Click Add New Risk
  3. Select Describe with AI
  4. Type something like: "Equipment failure causing production delays"
  5. Click Generate Risk
  6. Review AI suggestions and click Save
üí°
Pro Tip: Look for the ‚ú® sparkle icons next to fields. Clicking these will generate AI suggestions for that specific field, even in manual mode!

Add Controls to Reduce Risk

Controls are the actions, policies, or systems you put in place to reduce risk. Here's how to add them:

1

Open a Risk

Go to Risk Register and click on any risk to open its details.

2

Find Linked Controls

Scroll down to the Linked Controls section.

3

Add Controls

Either check boxes next to existing controls, or click the + button to create a new one. AI will suggest relevant controls based on your risk.

4

Watch Risk Decrease

As you link controls, you'll see the Residual Risk score decrease on the dashboard!

üõ°Ô∏è
Control Types Explained
  • Preventive: Stops risks before they happen (policies, access controls)
  • Detective: Finds risks after they occur (monitoring, audits)
  • Corrective: Fixes issues after detection (incident response)

Generate Your First Report

Share your risk management progress with stakeholders through professional reports:

1

Go to Reports

2

Create New Report

Click Create Report and choose from templates like Executive Summary, Risk Assessment, or Control Effectiveness.

3

Customize Content

Select which risks and data to include. Add your own commentary and recommendations.

4

Export or Share

Download as PDF for presentations or share directly with team members.
