üë• Team Seats Management

Team seats determine how many people can access your workspace. Here's how seat management works:

Understanding Seats

  • Seat Assignment: Each team member requires one seat
  • Pro Plan Base: Includes 3 seats in the base price ($29/month)
  • Additional Seats: $10/month per additional seat
  • Owner Seat: The workspace owner always has a seat (included)

How to Add Seats

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Billing tab
  2. In the Team Seats section, click Add Seats
  3. Enter the number of seats you want to add
  4. Review the new monthly cost
  5. Click Add Seats to confirm

How to Remove Seats

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Billing tab
  2. Click Remove Unused Seats
  3. Select how many seats to remove (only unused seats can be removed)
  4. Review the updated monthly cost
  5. Click Remove Seats to confirm
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: You can only remove unused seats. If all seats are assigned to team members, you'll need to remove team members first before reducing seats.

üí∞ Payment Methods

Dimeri supports secure payment processing through Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • Debit cards with card network logos
  • International cards (currency conversion applies)

How to Update Your Payment Method

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Billing tab
  2. In the Payment Method section, click Update
  3. Enter your new card details in the secure Stripe form
  4. Click Update Card to save
‚úì Secure Processing: All payment information is encrypted and securely processed by Stripe. Dimeri never stores your full card details.

‚¨ÜÔ∏è Upgrading & Downgrading

How to Upgrade to Pro

  1. Click Upgrade button anywhere in the app
  2. Or go to Profile ‚Üí Billing ‚Üí Change Plan
  3. Select the Pro plan
  4. Enter payment details
  5. Confirm upgrade
  6. Immediate access to Pro features!

How to Downgrade to Free

  1. Go to Profile ‚Üí Billing tab
  2. Click Cancel Subscription
  3. Optionally provide feedback
  4. Confirm cancellation
  5. You'll have Pro access until the end of your billing period
  6. After that, you'll be moved to the Free plan
üí° Good to Know: When downgrading, your data is safe! However, you'll need to ensure your usage fits within Free plan limits (3 risk registers, 25 controls, 2 team members).

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change my plan at any time?
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the end of your current billing period.
Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?
A: Your data is never deleted! However, if you exceed Free plan limits, you'll need to delete some items or upgrade again to access everything.
Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact support@dimeri.ai with your request and we'll be happy to help.
Q: Do you offer annual billing?
A: Currently, we offer monthly billing. Annual billing with a discount is coming soon! Contact us for Enterprise options.
Q: How do I add more team members?
A: First, ensure you have enough seats (add more if needed). Then, only the workspace owner can invite members by clicking the team icon in the header and selecting "Invite Members".
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) through our secure payment processor, Stripe.
Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Absolutely! All payments are processed securely through Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant). We never store your full card details on our servers.
Q: Can I export my data before cancelling?
A: Yes! You can export all your data (risks, controls, reports) at any time from Settings ‚Üí Security ‚Üí Export Data.
