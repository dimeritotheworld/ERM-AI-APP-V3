Frequently asked questions

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we prorate any differences.

Is there a free trial?

Your data is never deleted. If you downgrade and exceed plan limits, you'll have read-only access to existing data until you're within limits.
