Account & Settings Guide

Customize your Dimeri ERM experience, manage your account, and control your data privacy.

Account Settings

Access your account settings by clicking the Settings icon in the sidebar, or click your profile in the header dropdown.

Profile Information

Setting Description
Name Your display name shown throughout the app
Email Your account email (used for notifications)
Role Your job title or role in the organization
Avatar Your profile picture (auto-generated from initials)

Auto-Save

All changes are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. No need to click a save button!

Workspace Settings

Configure your organization's workspace:

Organization Name

The name of your company or team. Appears in reports and exports.

Industry

Your industry sector. Helps AI provide relevant risk suggestions.

Default Risk Register

Which register loads by default when you open the app.

Team Members

Manage users who have access to your workspace (Pro plan).

Notification Preferences

Control what alerts and reminders you receive:

Notification Description Default
Risk Review Reminders Alerts when risk reviews are due On
High Risk Alerts Immediate notification for critical risks On
Control Testing Due Reminders for upcoming control tests On
Weekly Summary Weekly email digest of risk status Off

Appearance Settings

Theme Options

Light Mode

Default bright theme for daytime use

Dark Mode

Easier on the eyes in low-light conditions

System

Follows your operating system preference

Display Options

  • Sidebar collapsed: Start with sidebar minimized
  • Compact mode: Reduce spacing for more content
  • Dashboard widgets: Choose which cards appear on dashboard
